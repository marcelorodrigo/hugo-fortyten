Welcome to **Hugo FortyTen**—the easiest way to make your Hugo blog look modern, responsive, and fun!

**How to begin:**

1. Clone the theme to your site's `themes/` directory.
2. Set `theme: "hugo-fortyten"` in `hugo.yaml`.
3. Run `hugo server` and start writing!

Explore the built-in partials, layouts, and Tailwind styling. The next sample post shows you how to make the theme truly yours!
